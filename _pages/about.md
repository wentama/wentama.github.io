I am a Data Scientist at [CTrees](https://ctrees.org/about), where I work on global carbon tracking using satellite data and machine learning. I hold a BS in [Data Science and Analytics](https://engineering.case.edu/computer-and-data-sciences) from [Case Western Reserve University](https://case.edu/) (CWRU). Before joining CTrees full-time, I interned at the organization as a Data Science and Remote Sensing Intern.

Previously, I worked with NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory ([JPL](https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/)) on the [UAVSAR](https://uavsar.jpl.nasa.gov/) Mission team, applying classification algorithms to wildfire detection using synthetic aperture radar (SAR) data. At CWRU, I conducted research with Professor [Sakshi Arya](https://sakshiarya.github.io) on decision-making in agricultural science using multi-armed bandit algorithms, and contributed to the Database Group under Professor [Yinghui Wu](https://yinghwu.github.io/index.html), where I developed interfaces to support data management in materials science research.


__Skills & Tools__: Python, R, SQL, Javascript, Unix, AWS EC2, AWS S3, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Data Science, GIS, Google Earth Engine, Ray Framework, Anyscale, GDAL, Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Remote Sensing, Geospatial Analysis, Machine Learning, Multi-Armed Bandit Algorithms, GUI design.
